Profile

An interdisciplinary scientist with an interest in developing minimally invasive methods to monitor host immunological status. A strong background in immunology and translational biology and over three years of pre and post-doctoral experience in spectroscopic methods and multivariate data analysis applied to a range of interdisciplinary research themes in surgical metabolic modelling, nutrition, microbial ecology, comparative metabolic profiling and mucosal immunology. Currently enrolled on the graduate MBBS course at Imperial College London.
